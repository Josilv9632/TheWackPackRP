THe WaCK PaCK Job Guide

This is the how to for all the jobs in our server so far.

Job Discriptions

SLAUGHTERER

Take the job at the Job Center - You will notice you will have some new markers on the map for that job. 1. Proceed to the Butchers Locker Room. 2. Proceed to Pickup (Chicken Coop) - Process (furthur inside the factory) - Bag (even further inside the factory) - Sell at Delivery Point (Clucken Bell).

FISHERMAN

Take the job at the Job Center - You will notice you will have some new markers on the map for that job. 1. Proceed to the Fishermen's Locker Room to go on duty. 2. Drive to the Fishing Area (get a boat and proceed to fishing area to collect fish) 3. Once you have enough fish Deliver your fish at the Delivery Point marked on your map.

MINER

Take the job at the Job Center - You will notice you will have some new markers on the map for that job. 1. Proceed to the Miner's Locker Room to go on duty. 2. Proceed to the the Rock marker on your map to start collecting rocks (INSIDE THE MINE NOT ON TOP OF MOUNTAIN). 3. Proceed to the washed rocks location to process your rocks. 4. Next goto the Smelting area on the map to smelt your collected rocks. 5. Sell everything at the (sell copper, sell diamond, sell gold, sell iron) locations.

BUS DRIVER

Take the job at the Job Center - You will notice you will have some new markers on the map for that job. 1. Proceed to the red marker that says BUS. Here you will get your bus. 2. Once you get your bus you can hit DEL while sitting in the drivers seat to "go on duty" . This will allow you to drive around and randomly people will pop up on your map to pick up. Also real players can call the bus system with the phone so just hit DEL to go off duty during that.

TRUCKER

Take the job at the Job Center - 1. Proceed to the yellow marker that says Walker Logistics. 2. Enter the marker by the tool box to go on duty. 3. Next marker will allow you to retrive your truck. Once you get your truck you can hit DEL while sitting in the drivers seat to "go on duty" . This will show you some new spots on your mini map that you need to drive to.

FUEL

Take the job at the Job Center. 1. Proceed to the Oil Refiner Locker Room. 2. Proceed to the Drill for oil spot on your map. 3. Next goto the Mix Refined Oil located on your map. 4. Deliver Gas at the the location on the map.

TAXI / UBER

Take the job at the Job Center - You will notice you will have some new markers on the map for that job. 1. Proceed to the yellow marker that says Taxi. Here you will get your taxi or use your personal vehicle. 2. Once you get your taxi you can hit DEL while sitting in the drivers seat to "go on duty" . This will allow you to drive around and randomly people will pop up on your map to pick up. Also real players can call the taxi system with the phone so just hit DEL to go off duty during that.
